How to Make Rice Krispies Treats!

These Rice Krispies Treats are gooey and perfectly sweet, if you make this recipe I doubt that you'll regret it!

INGREDIENTS NEEDED:

  • 1 1/2 sticks of unsalted butter
  • Two 10-oz bags of mini marshmallows
  • 3/4 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 8 1/2 cups of Rice Krispies cereal
  1. First, line a 9 by 13 baking pan in aluminum foil and then lightly grease the pan with melted/softened butter. Be sure to set aside 2 cups of marshmallows for later.
  2. Using a pot, melt your butter over medium/light heat and occasionally stir the butter to be sure that the butter isn't burning. When the butter starts to turn a golden brown and you begin to smell a nutty, chocolatey sort of scent take your pot off the stove.
  3. Add in your vanilla extract, your sugar, your marshmallows, and your salt.
  4. Put your pot back on the stove and stir everything in thoroughly. (Most preferably with a wooden spoon, but just use a spoon that won't melt when in contact with heat.) Be sure that the marshmallows are completely melted.
  5. Remove your pot from the stove and add the cereal into the mixture. Stir everything in completely.<
  6. Add in the reserved marshmallows and stir only until partially melted. Transfer the mixture to the pan you prepared earlier and spread the mixture out evenly.
  7. Leave the treats to cool for about an hour, then cut into small squares and enjoy!
