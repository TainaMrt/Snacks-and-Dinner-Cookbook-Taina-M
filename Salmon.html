How to make Salmon!

Salmon is a food loved by many, and after following this recipie I hope that it'll be loved even more!

INGREDIENTS NEEDED:

  • 4, 5-ounce center-cut salmon fillets (about 1-inch-thick
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Black pepper
  1. Be sure that the salmon is room temperature 10 minutes before cooking.
  2. Preheat a large nonstick skillet with oil over medium-ish heat. (any oil is fine)
  3. Season the fish with salt and pepper.
  4. Raise the heat to medium-high and put the salmon, skin-side up in the pan.
  5. Cook until golden brown on 1 side,for about 4-5 minutes. Turn the salmon over with a spatula, and cook for another 4-5 or until it feels firm to the touch. (If you prefer your skin to be crisp, cook for about 3 more minutes.)
  6. Transfer your food to a plate and enjoy!.
