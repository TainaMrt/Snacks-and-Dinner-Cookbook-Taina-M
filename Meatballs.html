How to make Meatballs!

This is a meal that people of all ages are bound to enjoy!

INGREDIENTS NEEDED:

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup italian-style bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped (1/4 cup)
  • 1 egg
  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F.
  2. Prep a 13 by 9 baking pan with foil and spray with cooking spray.
  3. In large bowl, mix all the ingredients together. Shape the meat into 24 medium-sized meatballs, and place them 1 inch apart on the baking pan.
  4. Bake the meatballs for 18 to 22 minutes or until the meatballs are no longer pink in the center/raw.
