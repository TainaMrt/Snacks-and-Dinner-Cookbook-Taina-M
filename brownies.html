How to Make Fudge Brownies!

These brownies are everything that a brownie should have; a nice fudgy middle with a crisp outside!

INGREDIENTS NEEDED:

  • 1 stick of melted butter
  • 1 tbsp of cooking oil
  • 1 1/8th cups of white granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsps of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp of salt
  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F
  2. Grease an 8-inch baking pan and line it with parchment paper
  3. Mix your melted butter, cooking oil, and white granulated sugar, together in a medium/large-sized bowl. Mix well.
  4. Add in your eggs and vanilla extract and beat for about a minute, or until lighter in color.
  5. Combine the all-purpose flour, cocoa powder, and salt and sift it into the wet ingredients, mix together but DO NOT mix all the way.
  6. Pour your brownie batter into your baking pan and tap it on the table to smooth out the batter. (Or you can just use a spatula to spread out the batter)
  7. Bake for about 20-25 minutes or until the middle part of the brownies in the pan stops jiggling. Leave the brownies to sit in the pan for a bit.
  8. Let the brownies cool off before cutting into them and you should have a nice fudgy middle! ENJOY!!
